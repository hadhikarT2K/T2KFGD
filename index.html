About Me

I am Dr. Haradhan Adhikary, a postdoctoral researcher specializing in experimental high-energy nuclear physics. My research explores Quark-Gluon Plasma, critical phenomena in heavy-ion collisions, and neutrino oscillation physics. I have contributed extensively to the NA61/SHINE collaboration and presented findings at numerous international conferences.
